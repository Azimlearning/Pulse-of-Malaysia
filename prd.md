# Project Requirements Document (PRD) - Pulse of Malaysia

## 1. Vision & North Star
**North Star**: To provide a data-driven "Pulse of Malaysia" that identifies economic vulnerability hotspots in Malaysia and predicts mental health risks (suicides) to inform targeted policy interventions.

## 2. Target Audience
- Government policymakers (MOH, DOSM)
- Mental health NGOs
- Social researchers

## 3. Core Features
- **Interactive Geospatial Dashboard**: Visualization of suicide cases (2023) and unemployment rates by state.
- **Economic Vulnerability Index (EVI)**: A composite score merging CPI, unemployment, and other stress indicators.
- **Risk Predictor/Simulator**: A tool to forecast risk based on economic trend shifts.
- **Policy Intervention Gaps**: Identifying districts with high risk but low mental health resource density.

## 4. SDG Alignment
- **SDG 3**: Good Health and Well-being (Target 3.4: Reduce mortality from non-communicable diseases and promote mental health).
- **SDG 8**: Decent Work and Economic Growth (Target 8.5: Full and productive employment and decent work for all).

## 5. Success Metrics
- **Accuracy**: Alignment with official DOSM/MOH statistics.
- **Usability**: Intuitive interactive filtering and clear risk communication.
- **Impact**: Demonstrated ability to identify at least 3 high-priority "interventions zones".

## 6. Constraints
- **Data Source**: Strictly official Malaysian data (OpenDOSM, MOH, KKMNow).
- **Timeline**: Data Challenge 5.0 submission deadlines.
- **Deployment**: Vercel (Publicly accessible URL).
